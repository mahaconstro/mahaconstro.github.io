Working process

1. Virtual Consultancy, Proposal, & Estimation

In the initial stage, we will connect virtually to discuss your needs and preferences. We will guide you in visualizing the design and understanding Vastu principles. Throughout the design, execution, and budgeting phases, we will provide positive and detailed proposals and estimates.

2. Work Order & Site Measurements

We invest significant effort in preparing our Proposal & Estimation. mahaconstro provides this information to facilitate your decision to hire us. Upon approval, we will finalize the Agreement & acquire the Work Order from you.

3. Concept Creation & Design Development

Once we receive the work order and complete the site measurements, we begin the Design phase. This entails creating 2D and 3D designs to meet your requirements and obtaining your approval. During this phase, we collaborate with you to develop working documents and a mood board, emphasizing the significance of Design services in initiating site work.

4. Site Management & Execution

Our dedicated Project Management team strives to turn your aesthetic dreams into reality. We maintain close collaboration with contractors, sub-contractors, and related personnel to ensure timely site visits and implementation of your specific requirements, ensuring your utmost satisfaction.

5. Site Completion & Reporting

"The project's completion marks a significant milestone, where we meticulously coordinate with relevant agencies to finalize all details. This includes tasks such as acquiring accessories, completing any necessary touch-ups, and carefully arranging furniture and artwork. Additionally, you will receive the Project Cost report and as-built Drawings."
